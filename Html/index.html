SM Foundation College Scholarship

Provider: SM Foundation · Updated: March 2025

YOUR MATCH SCORE

Great match! You qualify for most requirements.

📋 Requirements

  • • Good academic standing (GWA 2.0 or better)
  • • Filipino citizen enrolled in college
  • • Family annual income below ₱400,000

🎁 Benefits

  • • Full tuition coverage
  • • Monthly allowance (₱3,000/month)
  • • Part-time job opportunities within SM

📅 Application Period

January – April each year. Applications submitted online via the SM Foundation website.

📝 How to Apply

  1. 1 Visit the SM Foundation website at smfoundation.org
  2. 2 Click on 'Scholarships' and select 'College Scholarship Program'
  3. 3 Create an account or log in to your existing account
  4. 4 Fill out the online application form with personal and academic information
  5. 5 Upload required documents: birth certificate, income proof, grades transcript
  6. 6 Submit the application before the April deadline
  7. 7 Wait for email notification for interview scheduling
  8. 8 Attend the virtual or in-person interview
  9. 9 Receive scholarship award notification within 2-3 months

🛡️ Security Note

Always verify the official SM Foundation website. Never share sensitive information via email or unofficial channels.
